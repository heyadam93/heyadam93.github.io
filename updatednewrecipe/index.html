Guacamole Recipe

You will learn how to create the most simple and greatest Guacamole recipe to enjoy at get togethers, sports parties, family parties, and pretty much anywhere else! With less than $10, you can make Guacamole that can feed up to 3-5 people. Everyone knows that Guacamole can't be enjoyed without its savory partner, tortilla chips!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 1 Tbsp of fresh lime juice or lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp to 1/4 cup of chopped red onion or thinly sliced green onions
  • 1-2 Serrano chilis, remove stem and seeds
  • 2 Tbsp cilantro finely chopped
  • A dash of black pepper
  • 1/2 ripe tomato, remove seeds and pulp

Serve with tortilla chips
